About us

ACM ENSTAB is a club based in the National School of Advanced Sciences and Technologies of Borj Cedria (ENSTAB).

We aim to grow the community of competitive programmers by teaching competitive programming techniques and competing within the community.

ENSTA Challenge

ENSTA Challenge is a competitive programming contest for ENSTAB students.

During this competition, teams of 3 will be given a set of problems to try to solve them using programming languages (C/C++).
